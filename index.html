Öffi - your public transport companion!

Author: Andreas Schildbach

Features:

  • Discover nearby stations
  • Show departures
  • Show directions from A to B, save to your calendar or mail to a friend
  • View network plans
  • Widget for departures of nearby station favorite

Coverage:

  • Germany
  • Switzerland
  • Europe
  • Berlin - Brandenburg
  • Frankfurt - Rhein/Main
  • Munich

Requirements:

  • Android 1.5-2.2
  • Internet connectivity
